What is an Animal Health Certificate?

An Animal Health Certificate (AHC) is a mandatory document for transporting pets from the UK to the EU. It must be issued by an Official Veterinarian (OV) within 10 days of departure, valid for one EU trip, and for non-commercial travel, up to 5 pets. AHCs are for dogs, cats, and ferrets to the EU (Switzerland/Norway). Other animals or different destinations may need different certificates; contact us for guidance.
