Ready to Request?
  1. Click the button below to open up an email (or send an email to request@pettravelcertifyuk.com)
  2. In the email subject line please write your name, your mobile no., and your one preferred collection date
    Example: "ARTHUR MILLER, 07123 456789, 18/09/2023"
    (please note that there are no collections on Sundays & Bank Holidays).
  3. Attach your completed Download Request Form.
  4. Attach pictures of the required documents.
  5. Send! (You will receive an auto-responder acknowledgement to confirm receipt)

Please ensure you have fully read the information about the documents we are able to accept and the specific information that must be included if you are providing the evidence.
